Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide certain personally identifiable information, including:

  • Email address
  • First name and last name

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically and may include your device's IP address, device type, operating system, and diagnostic data.

Microphone & Audio

DayindAI uses your microphone to record voice answers during AI interview sessions. Audio recordings are processed by our AI services to provide feedback and are not stored permanently.

Use of Your Personal Data

  • To provide and maintain our Service

  • To contact you regarding updates or security notices
  • To manage your requests

Third-Party AI Services

DayindAI uses third-party Artificial Intelligence (AI) services to power its core features. When you use features such as AI Interview Practice, Resume Analyzer, Resume Matching, Visa Interview, or Cover Letter generation, certain data is transmitted to these third-party AI providers to generate responses.

What data is sent to AI services

Depending on the feature you use, the following data may be transmitted to third-party AI providers:

  • Text you type — job descriptions, resume text, interview answers entered via keyboard
  • Voice recordings — audio captured via microphone during AI Interview or Visa Interview sessions, converted to text (transcription) before being processed
  • Resume content — text extracted from uploaded resume files for analysis and matching
  • Interview conversation history — the sequence of questions and answers during an active interview session

We do not send your name, email address, or account credentials to any AI service.
